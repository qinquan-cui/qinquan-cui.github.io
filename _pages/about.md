Welcome! I am a fourth year PhD student at the [UCL School of Management](https://www.mgmt.ucl.ac.uk){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}, University College London. My research focuses on pharmaceutical markets using analytical models and large-scale empirical analysis. I study how institutional information dynamics and regulation shape pricing, marketing, and innovation, and I design policy and hospital-operations interventions that lower prices and expand access. <!--## My research is notably centered on understanding the intricate dynamics of pharmaceutical markets and the impact of legal frameworks on balancing the innovation, availability, and affordability of medicine. -->
